## Learning Goals

- Understand the roles of DNS, DHCP, NTP, and IPAM.
- Understand the basic purposes of Kerberos, RADIUS, LDAP, and SSO.
- Recognize how service failures affect security and business operations.

## Practice

- [ ] Use `nslookup` or `dig` to query A, AAAA, MX, and TXT records.
- [ ] Check the address assigned by local DHCP.
- [ ] Record why NTP matters for log analysis.
