## Learning Goals

- Understand the differences between WiFi, WLAN, WPA, WPA2, WPA3, and WEP.
- Understand WPS, EAP, PEAP, rogue access points, and evil twin attacks.
- Understand basic VPN use cases and common misconceptions.

## Safety Notes

Wireless security testing must only be performed in authorized environments.
