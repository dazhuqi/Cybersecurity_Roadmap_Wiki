# Glossary

This glossary is for terms I see repeatedly across modules. It should stay short enough to scan. If a term needs a full explanation, I should link to the full topic page instead of stuffing everything here.

## How to Add Terms

Add a term when:

- I see it in more than one module.
- I keep forgetting what it means.
- It appears in labs, tools, or certification notes.
- It helps connect multiple topics.

Keep descriptions beginner-friendly. If I cannot explain a term simply, that is a sign I need to study it more.
