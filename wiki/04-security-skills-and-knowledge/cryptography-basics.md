## Learning Goals

- Understand hashing, salting, key exchange, and PKI.
- Distinguish symmetric encryption from asymmetric encryption.
- Understand the relationship between public keys, private keys, certificates, and TLS.

## Review Questions

- What is the difference between hashing and encryption?
- Why should stored passwords use salts?
- What problem do certificates solve in HTTPS?
