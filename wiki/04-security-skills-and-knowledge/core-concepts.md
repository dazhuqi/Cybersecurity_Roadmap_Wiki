| Field | Value |
| --- | --- |

## Learning Goals

- Understand the CIA Triad: Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability.
- Understand risk, threat, vulnerability, impact, and likelihood.
- Understand defense in depth, isolation, zero trust, backups, and resiliency.
- Distinguish blue team, red team, and purple team roles.

## Review Questions

- What is the difference between risk and vulnerability?
- Why are backups part of security?
- What does zero trust actually mean?
