## Learning Goals

- Understand authorized testing and rules of engagement.
- Learn Kali Linux, ParrotOS, common tools, and exploit frameworks.
- Understand knowledge bases for dual-use tooling such as LOLBAS, GTFOBins, and WADComs.

## Safety and Ethics

Only test within clearly authorized scope. Record targets, scope, time windows, limitations, and evidence handling requirements.
