# Troubleshooting and Productivity Suites

| Field | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Status | Not Started |
| Roadmap Area | Fundamental IT Skills |

## Learning Goals

- Learn a general troubleshooting process: confirm symptoms, narrow scope, validate assumptions, and record conclusions.
- Understand security risks in common productivity suites such as MS Office, Google Suite, and iCloud.
- Understand how accounts, shared links, cloud sync, and permission settings can create risk.

## Practice

- [ ] Write a general troubleshooting checklist.
- [ ] Review the sharing permissions of a cloud document.
- [ ] Record a real or simulated troubleshooting case.
