## Learning Goals

- Identify core components such as CPU, memory, storage, motherboard, network card, and power supply.
- Understand connection types such as USB, HDMI, Ethernet, WiFi, Bluetooth, and NFC.
- Choose a reasonable troubleshooting direction for common hardware or connectivity issues.
